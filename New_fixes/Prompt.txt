SCHEMA RULES
- Preface -> sections -> subsections
- Divisions -> parts -> sections -> subsections -> clauses
- Conversion Factors -> sections
- Appendices stay under divisions
- No top-level chapters
- No top-level front_matter

NUMBERING RULES
Use BCBC numbering exactly:
- Part = 4
- Section = 4.1
- Subsection = 4.1.1
- Clause/Article = 4.1.1.3

So:
- 4 -> part
- 4.1 -> section
- 4.1.1 -> subsection
- 4.1.1.3 -> clause

Sentence items like 1), 2) stay inside clause.content.
Markers like (a), (b), (i), (ii) stay as structured content items inside clauses.
